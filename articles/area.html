Area

The term ‘area’ is a quantity giving the size of a two-dimensional region or shape within a given enclosed border.

The formulae for the area of common shapes are:

shape formula note
square where is the length of one of the sides
rectangle where is the base and is the height
circle = where is the radius
triangle where is the base and is the height
