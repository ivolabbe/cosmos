Upgrade #1 · Very High Impact

Interactive Visualisations

Replace static images with explorable 3D diagrams. A prototype already exists for the Zenith article.

Flagship Targets

  • HR Diagram — hover stars, see properties, click to articles
  • Galaxy Classification — explore the Hubble tuning fork
  • Cosmic Distance Ladder — step through each rung interactively
  • Celestial Sphere — adjustable latitude, rotating sky
  • EM Spectrum — slide through wavelengths, see telescopes
  • Stellar Evolution — animate a star's life cycle

Technical Approach

  • Three.js (3D) and D3.js (data viz) — no build step, CDN-loaded
  • Self-contained HTML+JS per visualisation
  • Embeddable in article pages or standalone
  • Mobile-responsive, touch-friendly
  • Each diagram links back to relevant COSMOS articles

Prototype: experimental/zenith-interactive.html

Upgrade #2 · High Impact, Low Effort

Knowledge Graph Explorer

COSMOS already has 644 densely cross-linked articles. Visualise the connections.

HyperPhysics pioneered concept-map navigation in 1998 — but with crude bubble diagrams frozen in time. We can build a modern, interactive force-directed graph where users visually explore how concepts connect.

Click "Black Hole" and see its connections radiate outward: Event Horizon, Singularity, Hawking Radiation, Neutron Star, Supernova, General Relativity...

Why This Works

  • Data already exists — just parse existing cross-links
  • Single page with D3.js force-directed layout
  • Click any node to read the article
  • Filter by letter, topic cluster, or search
  • Visually demonstrates the depth and interconnectedness of COSMOS
8 / 12
Upgrade #3 · High Impact, Low Effort

Structured Data & SEO

Make COSMOS machine-readable. Immediate SEO benefit, LLM integration, and app ecosystem.

JSON-LD Markup

Add Schema.org Article markup to every page. Enables Google Knowledge Panels and rich search results. The single highest-ROI SEO action available.

Cross-Link Graph

Export the article relationship graph as JSON. Apps, LLMs, and researchers can query how astronomy concepts connect — no other encyclopedia offers this.

Social Cards

OpenGraph and Twitter Card meta tags for every article. When someone shares a COSMOS link, it displays with title, description, and image — driving engagement.

9 / 12
Upgrade #4 · High Impact

Bridge Definitions & Current Research

Nobody links canonical astronomy definitions with the latest papers. COSMOS + NASA ADS can.

Each article gets a "Recent Research" sidebar, auto-populated from NASA ADS. A GitHub Actions workflow queries ADS weekly for papers matching each article's keywords.

The article on "Dark Energy" would show the 5 most recent relevant papers, with titles, authors, and links. No manual maintenance needed.

Why This Matters

  • Positions COSMOS as a living, current resource
  • Connects undergraduate learning with the research frontier
  • Auto-updated — no editorial burden
  • Unique: Astrobites covers daily papers but doesn't link to definitions. COSMOS would do both.
