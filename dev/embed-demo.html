COSMOS Interactive Demos

Interactive Visualisations

These interactive diagrams can be embedded in any COSMOS article page using a simple iframe. They replace static images with explorable 3D visualisations.

Asteroid Belt & Inner Solar System

Shows all five inner planets, the main asteroid belt with Kirkwood gaps, and Jupiter's Trojan asteroids at the L4 and L5 Lagrange points. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, hover for details.

Interactive: drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, hover labels for details.

Zenith & Celestial Sphere

Shows the celestial sphere from the observer's perspective, with zenith, nadir, celestial meridian, equator, and both celestial poles. Latitude is adjustable.

Interactive: drag to rotate, scroll to zoom. Adjust latitude with the slider.

Embedding in Articles

To add an interactive visualisation to any COSMOS article, use:

<div style="width:100%;max-width:800px;height:500px;border-radius:6px;overflow:hidden;margin:24px auto;box-shadow:0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.2);">
  <iframe src="../dev/asteroid-interactive.html"
    style="width:100%;height:100%;border:none;" loading="lazy">
  </iframe>
</div>
